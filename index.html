FNF: Friday Night Funkin' is an immersive music game with rhythm-based gameplay. Take the role of Boyfriend and join rap battles for love.

FNF: Join The Rhythm Showdown

Friday Night Funkin', also known as FNF, blends classic rhythm mechanics with catchy tracks and colorful stages. Each beat tests reflexes and timing through iconic duels.

Two Main Modes

  • Story: play themed weeks and finish all songs in order.
  • Freeplay: pick any track and enjoy at your own pace.

How To Play

  • Watch the arrows and press the corresponding keys in time with the beat.
  • Keep the health bar green by hitting accurate notes.

Why Fans Love It

Catchy OST, bold art style, and a huge mod community keep every session fresh. Challenge favorites like Daddy Dearest, Pico, Senpai, and more.
