Friday Night Funkin' is a rhythm-based music game where you battle to the beat against quirky opponents. Every note keeps you closer to victory!

FNF combines colorful visuals, catchy tunes, and tight gameplay mechanics. Players must match arrows in sync with the music to outperform their rival.

Game Modes

  • Story Mode: progress through all weeks and opponents.
  • Freeplay Mode: play your favorite songs anytime.

Controls

  • Use arrow keys to match the notes.
  • Keep your timing perfect to stay ahead of your opponent.
